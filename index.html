About Me

Hi, I’m Matthew, a full stack web developer in sunny Orange County, California. I’m a huge geek with a love for creating and building. What initially got me into coding was a passion to positively influence people’s lives. I believe technology and app creation specifically, is a powerful way to do that. After two years of learning to code on my own, I attended a coding bootcamp at the University Of California, Irvine. Since then I’ve learned a few important life lessons. Get comfortable with being uncomfortable, code everyday, and never stop learning!

I've created websites and apps using HTML, CSS, Javascript, jQuery, Node, Express, SQL, MongoDB and React.

I have a background in sales and marketing and a degree in the theatre arts. I am open to new opportunities and would love to speak with you about working together on a full-time or contract basis.
